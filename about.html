About Us

Reflections the best decoration items seller, since 1990.

Established in 1990 as acrylic fabricators with a modest production centre of 2000 square feet, the privately owned Reflections expanded slowly but steadily to include a second division dedicated to the supply of all kind of decorations to the hospitality industry.

Thanks to its commitment to providing the latest designs and quality items together with impeccable service and legendary reliability, Reflections today boasts a total of over 18,000 square feet of production and storage facilities, which caters to the most distinguished clientele in the gulf region and beyond.

  • Our acrylics division is spearheaded by a highly trained team and equipped with the latest computerized machinery including a laser cutting machine, a CNC Router, and heating chambers. We produce all sorts of display items for the hospitality industry, interior and architectural designs, furniture, domes, podiums, illuminated features, signage – all manufactured from raw acrylic sheets.
  • Our decoration division provides the latest and most elegant designs for New Year, Christmas, Easter, Halloween, Valentine's Day, Oktoberfest, St. Patrick, and other seasonal events. Not restricted or limited to the supply of decoration items, the division also undertakes complete in-house designing, in addition to arranging balloon and styrofoam art, bouncy castle rentals, and event wristbands.

Our clientele comprises a who’s who of the hospitality industry, a testament to our long-standing and superlative reputation
